# Overview

Charity Soul is a charitable organization dedicated to utilizing LST yield for donations to charitable organizations.

Charity soul works by having users hold our LST, charitySOL, in order to make real impact. LSTs give us a great way to generate yields and utilize that towards charitable donations. Since LSTs are essentially part of stakepools, we will be able to track and withdraw all yield that the LST produces and utilize that towards charity

Signing up to use our platform is really simple, just go to our website, connect your wallet, and sign a message proving ownership.
