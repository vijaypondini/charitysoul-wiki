# Instant donations

Users can choose to also donate to charitable organizations without holding any charitySOL on our website if they wish. We process these donations with 0 fees, and 100% of your contribution goes to the charity! 

You are able to select whether you can donate to one charity of your choosing, or alternatively, donate to all the charities/categories of your liking. We only allow a set list of tokens to be used to donate with, to avoid any potential swap price impacts on smaller cap currencies

If you want to donate to a particular charity that may not be on our list, please reach out to us and we can arrange a donation for you!
